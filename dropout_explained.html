How Dropout Works

During training, each neuron is randomly zeroed out with probability p. At test time, all neurons are active and outputs are scaled to compensate.

Training

Without Dropout

All neurons active -- prone to co-adaptation

Training

With Dropout (p=0.5)

Random neurons zeroed out each step

Test Time

No Dropout

All neurons active, no scaling needed

Dropout in Your Hybrid CBM

Dropout is applied only to the side channel's 512 input features -- not to the concept path
